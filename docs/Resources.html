Resources

R and R-Studio

R is a free open-source programming language that can be used for statistical analysis, data-simulation, graphing, and lots of other stuff. Another free program is R-studio, that provides a nice graphic interface for R. Download R first, then download R-studio. Both can run on PCs, Macs or Linux. Students will be learning R in the stats labs using the lab manual lab manual.

Additional R resources

  1. Google is great, Google your problem
  2. Stackoverflow is great, Google will often take you there because someone has already asked your question, and someone else has answered, usually many people have answered your question many ways.
